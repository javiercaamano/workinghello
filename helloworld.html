The Computer Science Organization

The CSO offers many opportunities for ESU Computer Science/Security students. We offer many practical opportunities for students to apply their CPSC knowledge in Scitech 352 from 2:00-3:00pm on Thursdays.

We offer things like:

-Microcontroller kits and computing kits during club hours that students work with

-Experience building

-Networking with your peers / Project opportunities

-Community service events / opportunities

-Trips to conferences

-Assistance with Computer Science / Security concepts

Why Join?

We're a rapidly-growing and active community who works towards a common goal of IT knowledge and experience.
